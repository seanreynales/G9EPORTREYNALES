2nd Quarter Activities

Teacher's Day



Teacher's Day this school year was somehow different. 10 of my classmates became mini teachers, in which they discussed various lessons according to their assigned subject. After a dance number and song number, we ate food from Shakeys. The teachers then played a minigame. And lastly, Ms. Gia performed the song "Part of Your World."




Historical Icon



Helping out in the making of cultural icon was amazing. I was part of the fabric-cutting department and the painting department. I really loved it when I was painting the paper roses. The other painters and I were tasked to paint the roses red and blue.




VPOP



Having V-POP again this school year was actually fun. In my opinion, our song choices were cool. I can say that the choreography was tiring, but we got used to it since we practiced that over and over.
